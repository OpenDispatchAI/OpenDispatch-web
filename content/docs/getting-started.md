# Getting Started

OpenDispatch lets you automate your iPhone using natural language. Instead of memorizing exact Siri commands, just say what you want — OpenDispatch figures out the rest.

## Installing Skills

1. Open OpenDispatch on your iPhone
2. Go to the Skills tab
3. Browse or search for a skill
4. Tap Install

Each skill adds a set of voice-controlled actions to your device.

## Using Skills

Once a skill is installed, just speak naturally. For example, with the Tesla skill installed:

- "Unlock my car"
- "Turn on the AC in my Tesla"
- "Set the temperature to 72"

OpenDispatch matches your words to the right action automatically.
